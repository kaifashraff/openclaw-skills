# Email Marketing

Build relationships and drive sales through strategic email campaigns.

## Email Types

### Newsletter
- Monthly updates
- New collection announcements
- Artisan stories
- Industry insights

### Promotional
- Festival sales
- Flash deals
- Wholesale offers
- Seasonal collections

### Transactional
- Order confirmations
- Shipping updates
- Abandoned cart reminders
- Thank you notes

## Subject Line Formulas

- `[Number] reasons to [benefit]` — "5 reasons to choose handcrafted zari"
- `Your [time-sensitive] guide to [topic]` — "Your wedding season style guide"
- `[Question]` — "Looking for unique bridal wear?"
- `[Emoji] [Short hook]` — "✨ New collection just dropped"
- `[Customer testimonial quote]` — "Your Sherwani made our wedding magical"

## Key Performance Indicators

- Open rate: 20-30% (industry avg: 15-20%)
- Click-through rate: 2-5%
- Conversion rate: 1-3%
- Unsubscribe rate: <0.5%

## Sending Schedule

| Email Type | Frequency |
|------------|-----------|
| Newsletter | Monthly |
| Promotional | 2-4 weeks before festival/event |
| Follow-up | 3 days, 7 days, 14 days after purchase |
| Re-engagement | After 60 days of inactivity |
