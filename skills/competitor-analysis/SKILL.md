# Competitor Analysis

Monitor and analyze competitors in the zari embroidery market.

- Competitor B: Mid-market, bulk orders focus
- Competitor C: Export-oriented, consistent quality

## Analysis Framework

### 1. Product Analysis
- What's their signature style?
- Price range and positioning?
- Quality indicators (materials, finishing)?
- Newest collections (what's trending)?

### 2. Online Presence
- Website: UX, product photos, SEO
- Social media: Followers, engagement, content strategy
- Reviews: What customers praise/criticize
- Pricing: Are they higher, lower, or similar?

### 3. Market Position
- Target audience: Boutiques, bridal, export, retail?
- Geographic focus: Local, national, international?
- Unique selling proposition: What do they emphasize?

## Monitoring Tools

- Google Alerts for competitor names
- Social media following (track their growth)
- Price comparison tools
- Review sites (Google, Justdial, Sulekha)
- Trade show attendance
