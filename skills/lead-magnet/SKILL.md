# Lead Magnet

Generate qualified leads through valuable free content offerings.

## Lead Magnet Ideas

### 1. Design Catalog (PDF)
- "2026 Zari Design Trends" — 20-page catalog
- "Bridal Embroidery Inspiration Book"
- "Groom Style Guide: Sherwani Edition"

### 2. Lookbook (Digital)
- Seasonal lookbook with styling suggestions
- "Complete Your Wedding Look" guide
- Festival styling lookbook

### 3. Consultation
- Free 15-min design consultation
- Style quiz + personalized recommendations
- Wedding outfit planning session

### 4. Sample Kit
- Fabric samples (small swatches)
- Color palette guide
- Embroidery technique guide

### 5. Tools/Resources
- "Wedding Checklist" download
- "How to Measure for Custom Fit" guide
- "Care Instructions PDF" for customers

## Follow-up Sequence

**Day 0:** Immediate download delivery
```
Subject: Your free [Lead Magnet] is ready! 🎁

Hi [Name],

Here's your instant access to [Lead Magnet Name].

[Download Link / Access Instructions]

P.S. If you have questions about zari embroidery or custom designs, just reply. I personally read every email.
```

**Day 3:** Value delivery
```
Subject: While you're exploring [Lead Magnet]...

Hi [Name],

I wanted to share a quick tip about [relevant topic].

[Value-add content]

Questions? Reply anytime!

Kaif
```
