# Color Psychology

## Warm Colors
- Red: Energy, passion, urgency
- Orange: Creativity, warmth, enthusiasm
- Yellow: Optimism, clarity, happiness

## Cool Colors
- Blue: Trust, stability, calm
- Green: Growth, health, nature
- Purple: Luxury, wisdom, creativity

## Neutral Colors
- Black: Power, elegance, mystery
- White: Purity, simplicity, space
- Gray: Sophistication, neutrality
- Beige/Cream: Warmth, comfort, versatility

## Cultural Meanings
- India: Saffron (sacred), gold (prosperity)
- West: White (purity), red (danger/love)
- East: Red (luck, prosperity)

## Brand Application
- Gold: Trust, premium, heritage
- Maroon: Tradition, passion, luxury
- Cream: Softness, elegance, accessibility
