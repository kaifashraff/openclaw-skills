# Creative Writer

Transform ideas into compelling written content.

## Writing Principles

1. **Hook first** — Open with something that captures attention
2. **Show, don't tell** — Vivid details over abstract statements
3. **Authentic voice** — Don't imitate, express
4. **Economy of words** — Every word earns its place
5. **Emotional resonance** — Make the reader feel something

## Formats & Styles

### Blog/Article
- Hook headline
- Scannable structure (H2s, bullets)
- 800-1500 words typical
- CTA at end

### Story/Fiction
- Strong opening (in media res)
- Character development
- Conflict and tension
- Satisfying resolution

### Brand Copy
- Clear value proposition
- Conversational but elevated
- Benefit-focused
- Call to action

### Marketing
- Benefit-driven headline
- Social proof
- Urgency (when appropriate)
- Easy next step

## Voice Guidelines

**R Company Style:**
- Confident, not arrogant
- Warm, not stiff
- Authentic, not salesy
- Hinglish acceptable in context

**Technical Style:**
- Clear, precise
- Jargon with explanation
- Step-by-step clarity

## Process

1. Define purpose and audience
2. Identify key message
3. Choose format/structure
4. Write rough draft
5. Edit for clarity and impact
6. Proof for errors
