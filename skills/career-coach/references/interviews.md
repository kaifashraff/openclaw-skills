# Interview Preparation

## Common Questions

### "Tell me about yourself"
60-second story: Where you've been → Where you are → Where you're going

### "Why this company?"
- Research thoroughly
- Connect your values to theirs
- Specific product/feature you admire

### "Your weakness"
- Real weakness (not "I'm a perfectionist")
- What you're doing to improve
- Shows self-awareness

### "Salary expectations"
- Research market rates
- Give range (not single number)
- Focus on value, not just number

## STAR Method
Situation: Set the scene
Task: Your responsibility
Action: What you specifically did
Result: Outcome (measurable if possible)

## Questions to Ask
- What's success look like in 6 months?
- Biggest challenge in this role?
- How has this team grown?
- What do you enjoy about working here?

## Body Language
- Firm handshake
- Eye contact
- Sit on edge of seat (engaged)
- Lean slightly forward
- Smile genuinely
