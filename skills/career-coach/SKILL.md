# Career Coach

Navigate professional growth and career decisions.

## Self-Assessment

### Skills Inventory
- What are you naturally good at?
- What have you learned recently?
- What do others ask for help with?
- What do you enjoy doing?

### Values
- What matters most? (Money, impact, freedom, security, growth)
- Non-negotiables?
- Deal-breakers?

### Motivations
- Intrinsic: Autonomy, mastery, purpose
- Extrinsic: Salary, status, recognition

## Career Frameworks

### 30-60-90 Plan
- 30 days: Learn (understand, ask questions, observe)
- 60 days: Contribute (apply knowledge, solve problems)
- 90 days: Lead (initiate projects, influence)

### Career Ladders vs Lattices
- Ladder: Vertical growth (junior → senior → manager)
- Lattice: Multi-directional (skills, roles, industries)

## Job Search

### Resume
- One page (2 max)
- Impact = Verb + Metric + Context
- Customize per application
- ATS-friendly keywords

### Interview
- Research company deeply
- Prepare stories (STAR: Situation, Task, Action, Result)
- Questions to ask interviewer (shows preparation)
- Follow up with thank you note

### Networking
- It's about relationships, not transactions
- Give before asking
- Stay in touch
- 80% of jobs come through connections

## Skill Building

### Learning Strategy
- Pick 1-2 skills per quarter
- Learn by doing (projects > courses)
- Teach to reinforce
- Apply within 48 hours

### High-Value Skills
- Communication (written + verbal)
- Problem-solving
- Technical in your domain
- Relationship building

## Career Transitions

1. Transferable skills first
2. Build before jumping
3. Network in new field
4. Expect 6-12 months of transition
