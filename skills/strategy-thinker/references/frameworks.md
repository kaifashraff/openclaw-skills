# Strategic Frameworks

## OKRs (Objectives & Key Results)
- Objective: Qualitative, aspirational
- Key Results: Quantitative, measurable
- Timeline: Quarterly
- Review: Weekly check-ins

## Scenario Planning
- Best case
- Most likely
- Worst case
- Contingency plans

## Game Theory
- Zero-sum vs positive-sum
- Nash equilibrium
- First-mover vs second-mover advantage
- Prisoner's dilemma analysis

## Blue Ocean Strategy
- Eliminate: What to stop doing
- Reduce: Less of what
- Raise: More of what
- Create: What's never been done
