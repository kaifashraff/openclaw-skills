# Strategy Thinker

Long-term thinking and decision frameworks.

## Strategic Framework

### 1. Define the Objective
- What are we trying to achieve?
- Success metrics?
- Timeline?

### 2. Understand the Context
- Current situation analysis
- Stakeholder mapping
- Resource assessment
- Constraints identification

### 3. Generate Options
- Multiple approaches
- Innovative alternatives
- Risk/reward for each

### 4. Evaluate & Decide
- Decision matrix
- Risk analysis
- Trade-off assessment
- Stakeholder buy-in

### 5. Execute & Iterate
- Action plan
- Milestones
- Feedback loops
- Pivot if needed

## Strategic Lenses

### SWOT Analysis
- Strengths: Core advantages
- Weaknesses: Areas needing improvement
- Opportunities: External chances to pursue
- Threats: External risks

### Porter's Five Forces
- Competition intensity
- New entrant threat
- Supplier power
- Buyer power
- Substitute threat

### BCG Matrix
- Stars: High growth, high share
- Question marks: High growth, low share
- Cash cows: Low growth, high share
- Dogs: Low growth, low share

## Decision Making

### When to Decide
- Reversible: Decide fast, change if wrong
- Irreversible: Take time, gather data

### Decision Quality
- Gather diverse perspectives
- Challenge assumptions
- Pre-mortem: "What could go wrong?"
- Commit once decided
