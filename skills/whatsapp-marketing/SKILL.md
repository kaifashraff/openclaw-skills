## Group Broadcast Strategy

### Welcome Message
```
Welcome to R Company's VIP Group! 🎊

Here you'll get:
🔹 First access to new collections
🔹 Exclusive wholesale prices
🔹 Behind-the-scenes artisan updates
🔹 Festival sale alerts

No spam, just premium updates. Feel free to share your requirements anytime!
```

### Broadcast Frequency
- New collection: 1/week
- Festival sale: 2-3 days before
- Artisan updates: 2/month
- Thank you/referral: As needed

## Best Practices

- Personalize with buyer's name
- Use emoji strategically (1-3 per message)
- Keep messages under 500 characters
- Include clear CTA (reply, call, visit link)
- Respond within 2 hours
- Use product photos (not just text)
- Track responses with labels
