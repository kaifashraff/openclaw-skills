**Always Include:**
- 1-2 branded (#RCompanyZari #LaqabV2)
- 2-3 niche-specific (zari work, hand embroidery)
- 1-2 broad reach (Indian fashion, ethnic wear)
- 1-2 seasonal/trending (wedding season, festival name)

**Total: 5-10 hashtags per post**

## Do's and Don'ts

✅ DO: Research before using (check competitor hashtags)
✅ DO: Rotate sets (same hashtags = shadowban risk)
✅ DO: Use location: #Ahmedabad #Mumbai #Delhi #India
❌ DON'T: Use banned hashtags (search first)
❌ DON'T: Mix unrelated tags
❌ DON'T: Use >15 (looks spammy)
❌ DON'T: Use only mega-tags (#fashion has 500M+ posts)
