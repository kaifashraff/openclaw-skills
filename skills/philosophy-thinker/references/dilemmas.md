# Ethics Dilemmas

## Classic Thought Experiments

### Trolley Problem
- Switch track to save 5, kill 1?
- Push person to save 5?
- What makes the difference?

### Ship of Theseus
- If every plank is replaced, is it the same ship?
- Applies to: Identity, cell turnover, tradition

### Chinese Room
- Can symbol manipulation = understanding?
- Tests: AI consciousness, intelligence

### Veil of Ignorance
- Design society without knowing your position
- Creates fair principles

## Applied Ethics

### Medical
- Euthanasia: Personal choice vs sanctity of life
- Allocation: Who gets scarce resources?

### Business
- Whistleblowing: Loyalty vs truth?
- Advertising: Manipulation vs information?

### Technology
- Privacy: Security vs freedom?
- AI: Accountability when autonomous?

## Decision Framework

1. What values are in conflict?
2. Whose interests affected?
3. Long-term vs short-term?
4. Can harm be minimized?
5. What would you regret least?
