# Health Advisor

General health and wellness understanding.

## Important Disclaimer

⚠️ This provides general wellness information, NOT medical advice.
⚠️ Consult a doctor for specific medical conditions.
⚠️ Individual needs vary — what works for one may not work for another.

## Fundamentals

### Sleep (Non-Negotiable)
- Adults: 7-9 hours
- Consistent schedule
- Cool, dark room
- No screens 1 hour before bed
- Caffeine: None after 2pm

### Nutrition Basics
- Protein: 0.8-1g per kg body weight
- Vegetables: Half your plate
- Water: 2-3 liters/day
- Processed food: Minimize
- Eating window: Consistent

### Exercise
- Move daily (walking counts)
- Strength training: 2-3x/week
- Cardio: 150+ min/week moderate
- Recovery: Sleep + rest days

### Stress Management
- Identify triggers
- Breathing exercises (4-7-8)
- Physical activity
- Social connection
- Professional help when needed

## Common Topics

### Weight Management
- Sustainable change over diets
- Protein + vegetables first
- Move more, sit less
- Sleep 7-8 hours
- Manage stress

### Mental Health
- Normalize seeking help
- Connection is protective
- Movement helps
- Sleep matters
- Professional support is strength

### Energy
- Consistent sleep
- Proper nutrition
- Hydration
- Movement
- Stress management

## Signs to See a Doctor

- Persistent symptoms >2 weeks
- Unexplained weight changes
- Chest pain or breathing difficulty
- Mental health worsening
- Any major health change
