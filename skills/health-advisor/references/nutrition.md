# Nutrition Guide

## Macronutrients

### Protein
- Sources: Dal, eggs, chicken, fish, paneer, tofu, nuts
- Function: Muscle repair, enzyme production
- Need: 0.8-1g per kg body weight

### Carbohydrates
- Sources: Rice, roti, vegetables, fruits
- Function: Energy
- Choose complex carbs over simple

### Fats
- Sources: Ghee, oil, nuts, avocado
- Function: Hormone production, absorption
- Limit: Trans fats, excessive saturated

## Micronutrients (Common Deficiencies)

### Iron
- Sources: Spinach, jaggery, lentils, meat
- For: Energy, immunity
- Vitamin C helps absorption

### Vitamin D
- Sources: Sunlight, fortified milk, eggs
- For: Bone health, immunity
- Many are deficient (get tested)

### B12
- Sources: Eggs, dairy, meat, supplements
- For: Nerve function, energy
- Often low in vegetarians

## Simple Plate Method
- 1/2 vegetables
- 1/4 protein
- 1/4 carbs
- Hand-sized portions
