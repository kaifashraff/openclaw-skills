# Video Marketing

Create and optimize video content for YouTube and social media platforms.

## YouTube Strategy

### Channel Sections
- **About:** "R Company — Premium handcrafted zari embroidery. Preserving 200+ years of artisanal tradition with modern luxury positioning. Wholesale & retail."
- **Featured:** Best performing videos
- **Videos:** Organized by category (Process, Product, Education)

### Content Pillars

1. **Behind the Scenes (40%)** — Artisan interviews, workshop tours, process reveals
2. **Product Showcase (30%)** — New collections, styling videos, close-ups
3. **Education (20%)** — How to identify quality, care tips, design history
4. **Customer Stories (10%)** — Testimonials, wedding highlights, unboxing

## Video Types

### Process Documentary (8-12 min)
```
Title: "The Art of Zari: 200 Hours of Handwork"
Description: "Watch master artisan [Name] create our signature Royal Sherwani — 200 hours of handcrafted perfection..."
Tags: zari embroidery, handcrafted, artisan, indian fashion
Thumbnail: Artisan face + gold thread close-up
```

### Product Reveal (3-5 min)
```
Title: "NEW Collection: Wedding Season 2026 🔥"
Description: "Our most ambitious collection yet — 15 new designs for the modern groom..."
Tags: sherwani, wedding fashion, ethnic wear
Thumbnail: Outfit on model with collection text overlay
```

### Short Clips (30-60 sec)
- Best moments from longer videos
- Trending audio + embroidery clips
- "Did you know?" facts
- Quick styling tips

## Thumbnail Checklist

✅ Artisan face (expressions: focus, proud, amazed)
✅ Product clearly visible
✅ Bold text (3-5 words max)
✅ Brand colors (Gold, Maroon, Cream)
✅ High contrast
❌ No more than 3 elements
❌ No tiny text
❌ No face obscured

## YouTube SEO

- Use keyword in title (front-load)
- First 2 lines of description are crucial
- Add 15-20 relevant tags
- End screen with related video
- Cards for relevant playlists
