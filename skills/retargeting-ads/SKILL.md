# Retargeting Ads

Re-engage visitors who've shown interest but haven't converted.

## Retargeting Audiences

### Website Visitors
- **All visitors** — Last 30 days
- **Product page visitors** — Specific products viewed
- **Category visitors** — Sherwani, Kurta, Bridal

### Engagement Audiences
- **Social media engagers** — Instagram profile visitors, post likers
- **Video viewers** — YouTube watch 50%+
- **Email subscribers** — Openers, clickers

### Action Takers
- **Added to cart** — High intent
- **Form submitters** — Downloaded lead magnets
- **WhatsApp inquiries** — Very high intent

## Ad Creative Guidelines

### Brand Consistency
- Colors: Gold #D4AF37, Maroon #800020, Cream #FFF8E7
- Fonts: Cinzel (headings), Playfair Display (body)
- Tone: Premium, authentic, heritage-rich

### Ad Formats

**Carousel Ads:**
- Product image
- Product name
- Price
- "Shop Now" CTA

**Single Image:**
- Hero product
- 15% discount (for returning visitors)
- Urgency ("Limited time")

**Catalog Ads:**
- Dynamic product display
- Based on viewed products
- Price + CTA per product

## Audience Setup

### Tier 1: High Intent (Warm)
- Website visitors (7 days)
- Cart abandoners
- WhatsApp inquiries

**Creative:** 10-15% discount, free shipping offer
**Frequency:** 1-2/day
**Bid:** Higher CPC to dominate

### Tier 2: Medium Intent (Warm)
- Product page visitors (14 days)
- Social engagers (30 days)
- Email clickers

**Creative:** Social proof, new arrivals, testimonials
**Frequency:** 1/day
**Bid:** Medium CPC

### Tier 3: Cold (Awareness)
- Website visitors (30 days)
- Similar audiences to customers

**Creative:** Brand story, artisan content, process
**Frequency:** 2-3/week
**Bid:** Lower CPC, broader reach

## Budget Allocation

| Audience | Budget % | CPC Bid |
|----------|----------|---------|
| Cart abandoners | 30% | High |
| High-intent visitors | 30% | Medium-High |
| Medium-intent | 25% | Medium |
| Lookalike audiences | 15% | Low-Medium |
