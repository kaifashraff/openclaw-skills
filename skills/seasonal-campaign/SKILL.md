# Seasonal Campaign

Plan and execute high-impact seasonal marketing campaigns.

## Annual Calendar

### January-February
- **Post-wedding season recovery** — Thank you campaigns, testimonial collection
- **Valentine's Day** — Couple-themed content (not primary market)

### March-May
- **Holi** (March) — Light zari, care tips, spring collection
- **Summer collection** — Lightweight zari for warmer months

### June-August
- **Father's Day** (June) — Gifting guide, men's ethnic
- **Monsoon** — Care tips, lightweight options
- **Raksha Bandhan** (August) — Sibling gifting, matching sets

### September-November (PEAK SEASON)
- **Navratri** (Sep-Oct) — Garba wear, daily color themes
- **Wedding Season** (Oct-Feb) — Maximum activity, daily posts
- **Diwali** (Oct-Nov) — Heavy promotion, corporate gifting

### December
- **Christmas/New Year** — Year-end sale, gifting
- **Wedding Season continues** — Maintain momentum

## Wedding Season Campaign (Oct-Feb)

**Duration:** 5 months
**Focus:** Grooms, bridal parties, wedding functions
**Content Mix:**
- 40% Product showcases
- 30% Testimonials/social proof
- 20% Behind-the-scenes
- 10% Educational (styling tips)

**Key Messages:**
- "Made for your most important day"
- "200+ brides trust R Company"
- "Custom designs for your wedding vision"

## Diwali Campaign

**Timeline:** 3-4 weeks before
**Messaging:** Gifting, family, tradition, celebration

**Content Plan:**
- Week -4: Teaser ("Something special coming...")
- Week -3: Collection reveal
- Week -2: Gift guide
- Week -1: Last-minute gifting
- Week 0: Live shopping/flash sale

**Special Elements:**
- Corporate gifting inquiry form
- Bulk order discounts
- Gift wrapping service
- Express delivery option
