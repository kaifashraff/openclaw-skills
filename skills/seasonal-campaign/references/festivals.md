# Festival Campaign Strategies

## Diwali Campaign (October-November)

**Timeline:** 4 weeks
**Goal:** ₹X revenue, Y new wholesale buyers

### Week-by-Week

**Week -4: Tease**
- "Something magical is coming..." (no product reveal)
- Countdown posts (7 days)
- Behind-the-scenes of preparation

**Week -3: Collection Launch**
- Full collection reveal (Reels + Carousel)
- Influencer partnerships (if budget)
- WhatsApp catalog update

**Week -2: Gift Guide**
- "Diwali Gift Guide for Everyone"
- Corporate gifting focus
- Testimonial posts with festive backgrounds

**Week -1: Urgency**
- "Only X days left!"
- Flash sale announcements
- Last order dates for delivery

**Diwali Week:** 
- Festival greeting (all channels)
- User-generated content (share your R Company moment)
- Thank you posts

### Budget Allocation
- Content creation: 30%
- Paid promotion: 40%
- Influencer: 20%
- Contests/giveaways: 10%

## Wedding Season Campaign (October-February)

**Duration:** 5 months
**Focus:** Grooms, bridal shops, wedding functions

### Monthly Content Themes

**October:** "Wedding Season Begins" — New sherwani collection
**November:** "The Perfect Wedding Outfit" — Sherwani focus
**December:** "New Year, New Collection" — Year-end celebration
**January:** "Wedding Season Peak" — Maximum posting
**February:** "Final Push" — Clearance, early bird for next season

### Key Campaigns

1. **"Groom's Guide" Series** (October)
   - 5-part educational series
   - "How to Choose Your Sherwani"
   - "What Makes Quality Zari"
   - "Styling Your Wedding Outfit"
   - "Care Tips for Bridal Wear"
   - "Custom vs Ready-Made"

2. **"Bridal Shop Partnership"** (November-January)
   - Exclusive wholesale pricing
   - Priority production for bulk
   - Custom branding options
   - Dedicated account manager

3. **"Real Wedding Stories"** (January-February)
   - Customer testimonials
   - Wedding photos/videos
   - Before/after transformations
   - Customer spotlight posts

## Eid Campaign (April-May, June-July)

**Duration:** 2 weeks
**Focus:** Elegant, festive, gift-ready positioning

### Content Strategy
- "Elegant for Eid" — Styling tips
- "Gift Guide" — Perfect presents
- "Customer Stories" — Eid celebrations
- "Limited Edition" — Festive collection

### Message Template
"This Eid, celebrate in handcrafted luxury. Our artisans have created exclusive pieces that blend tradition with modern elegance. Order now for delivery before Eid. 🎁"
