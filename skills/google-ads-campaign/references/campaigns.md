# Google Ads Campaign Setups

## Campaign 1: Brand Awareness (Festival Season)

**Objective:** Reach buyers searching for ethnic wear during peak season

**Campaign Settings:**
- Network: Search only
- Locations: India (priority), UAE, USA, UK
- Languages: English, Hindi
- Budget: ₹1,500/day

**Ad Group Structure:**
- Ad Group 1: Sherwani (₹600/day)
- Ad Group 2: Kurta Sets (₹400/day)
- Ad Group 3: Bridal Wear (₹500/day)

**Keywords:**
- Wedding sherwani
- Bridal lehenga wholesale
- Ethnic wear for men
- Festival collection ethnic

## Campaign 2: Remarketing (Past Visitors)

**Objective:** Re-engage website visitors who didn't convert

**Audience:**
- Website visitors (last 30 days)
- Social media engagers
- WhatsApp inquiries (no order)

**Ads:**
- Display retargeting with product images
- "Complete your purchase" messaging
- Special discount offers (5-10%)

**Budget:** ₹300/day

## Campaign 3: Shopping Ads (Product Feed)

**Setup:**
- Upload product feed to Google Merchant Center
- Include: Product title, price, image, category
- Target: India, International

**Ad Examples:**

## Campaign 4: Local Search (Ahmedabad)

**Target:** Local buyers in Gujarat

**Keywords:**
- zari embroidery ahmedabad
- ethnic wear shop ahmedabad
- wholesale ethnic clothes Gujarat
- sherwani ahmedabad

**Ad Extensions:**
