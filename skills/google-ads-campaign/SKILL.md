# Google Ads Campaign

Set up and manage effective Google Ads campaigns for R Company.

## Campaign Types

### Search Ads
- High intent buyers searching for products
- Keyword-based targeting
- Text ads with CTAs

### Display Ads
- Brand awareness, remarketing
- Visual banners
- Reach audiences who've visited your site

### Shopping Ads
- Product feed-based
- Show images + prices in search results
- Best for e-commerce

## Keyword Strategy

### Primary Keywords
- zari embroidery
- handcrafted ethnic wear
- sherwani wholesale
- bridal wear wholesale
- indian ethnic clothing
- kurta set online

### Long-tail Keywords
- handcrafted zari sherwani for wedding
- wholesale zari embroidery ahmedabad
- custom bridal wear india
- premium ethnic wear online shop

### Match Types
- Exact: [handcrafted zari embroidery] — High intent
- Phrase: "handcrafted ethnic wear" — Medium intent
- Broad: handcrafted ethnic clothing — Broad reach

## Quality Score Tips

- Relevant ad copy to keywords
- Fast-loading landing pages
- Clear CTAs on landing pages
- Proper ad extensions (sitelinks, callouts)
- Mobile-optimized experience

## Conversion Tracking

- Add tracking pixel to Laqab v2
- Set up conversion actions:
  - Form submission (contact inquiries)
  - WhatsApp click
  - Order placed
- Monitor cost per conversion
