# ROI Calculation

## Marketing ROI Formula

```
ROI = (Revenue - Cost) / Cost × 100
```

## Example Calculations

### Email Campaign
- Campaign cost: ₹500 (design + sending)
- Orders generated: 5
- Average order value: ₹10,000
- Revenue: ₹50,000
- ROI: (50000 - 500) / 500 × 100 = **9,900%**

### Social Media Post
- Content creation: ₹1,000
- Boost/sponsorship: ₹500
- Direct orders: 2 × ₹8,000 = ₹16,000
- ROI: (16000 - 1500) / 1500 × 100 = **967%**

### Google Ads
- Ad spend: ₹5,000
- Conversions: 8
- Average order: ₹12,000
- Revenue: ₹96,000
- ROI: (96000 - 5000) / 5000 × 100 = **1,820%**

## Break-Even Calculation

```
Break-even Revenue = Marketing Cost / Margin %
```

Example:
- Marketing cost: ₹10,000
- Product margin: 40%
- Break-even: ₹10,000 / 0.40 = ₹25,000 revenue needed

## Customer Lifetime Value

For repeat buyers:
```
LTV = Average Order Value × Purchase Frequency × Customer Lifespan
```

Example:
- Avg order: ₹10,000
- 2 orders/year
- 5 year relationship
- LTV: ₹10,000 × 2 × 5 = ₹100,000

This helps justify higher customer acquisition costs.
