## Buyer Types & Approaches

### Boutiques
- Focus: Unique designs, limited quantities
- Mention: Exclusivity, custom branding options
- Price sensitivity: Medium

### Bridal Shops
- Focus: Bulk orders, consistency, reliability
- Mention: Quality保证, on-time delivery, repeat business
- Price sensitivity: Low (quality matters)

### Export Buyers
- Focus: Quality standards, compliance, consistency
- Mention: International shipping, packaging, lead times
- Price sensitivity: Depends on market

### Individual Customers
- Focus: Emotional connection, heirloom quality
- Mention: Story behind craft, personalization
- Price sensitivity: High (need value justification)

## Relationship Building

- Remember their birthday/business anniversary
- Send festival greetings with small discount offers
- Share behind-the-scenes content occasionally
- Ask for feedback after orders
- Request testimonials and reviews
