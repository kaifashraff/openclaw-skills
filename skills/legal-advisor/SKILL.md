# Legal Advisor

Understand legal concepts and their practical implications.

## Important Disclaimer

⚠️ This provides general legal understanding, NOT legal advice.
⚠️ For specific legal matters, consult a qualified attorney.
⚠️ Laws vary by jurisdiction and change over time.

## Core Areas

### Contract Basics
- Offer and acceptance
- Consideration (something of value exchanged)
- Capacity (legally able to contract)
- Legality (lawful purpose)
- Written vs oral contracts

### Business Law
- Business formation (sole prop, LLC, partnership)
- Corporate structure
- Employment law basics
- Tax obligations

### Intellectual Property
- Copyright: Creative works (life + 70 years)
- Trademark: Brand identifiers (renewable)
- Patent: Inventions (20 years from filing)
- Trade secret: Confidential business info

### Common Business Contracts
- Service agreements
- NDAs (Non-Disclosure)
- Employment contracts
- Lease agreements
- Sales agreements

## Contract Essentials

### Must-Have
- Parties identified clearly
- Terms specific and measurable
- Consideration stated
- Signatures
- Date

### Watch Out For
- Vague language
- One-sided terms
- Missing termination clauses
- Unclear payment terms
- No dispute resolution

## Intellectual Property for R Company

- Product designs: Copyright + design registration
- Brand name/logo: Trademark
- Fabric patterns: Design patent or trade secret
- Customer data: Trade secret + privacy policy

## When to Get a Lawyer

- Complex contracts (>₹1L)
- Employment matters
- IP registration
- Dispute resolution
- Regulatory compliance
