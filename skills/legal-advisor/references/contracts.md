# Contract Clauses Guide

## Essential Clauses

### Payment Terms
- Amount (₹)
- Timing (advance, on delivery, net 30)
- Method (bank transfer, cheque)
- Penalties for late payment

### Termination
- Notice period required
- Grounds for immediate termination
- Effect on pending obligations

### Confidentiality
- What's confidential
- Duration of obligation
- Exclusions (public knowledge)

### Dispute Resolution
- Governing law (Indian law typically)
- Dispute method (negotiation, arbitration, court)
- Jurisdiction for legal proceedings

## Red Flags

- "Either party may terminate at any time" without notice
- Unlimited liability
- Indemnification for your actions
- Non-compete overbroad (geography, time)
- Auto-renewal without clear notice
