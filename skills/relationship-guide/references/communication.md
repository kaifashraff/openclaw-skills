# Communication Scripts

## Difficult Conversations

### Address a Problem
"I wanted to talk about something important. When [situation], I felt [emotion]. I'd like [specific request]. Can we discuss?"

### Receive Feedback
"Thank you for telling me. Can I take a moment to think, then we can discuss?"

### Apologize Sincerely
"I was wrong when I [specific action]. I understand it caused [impact]. I'm sorry, and here's what I'll do differently: [action]."

## Setting Boundaries

### Decline Request
"I appreciate you thinking of me. I can't commit to [request] right now because [reason]. Could we [alternative]?"

### When Pressured
"I've made my decision. I understand you may disagree, and that's okay. I'm not going to change this."

## Asking for Help

"Could I ask a favor? I need help with [specific thing]. Would you be able to [specific action]? I can [what you offer in return]."

## Breaking Up/Ending Relationships

Be direct but kind. "Our relationship has changed, and I don't think we should continue. I wish you well."
