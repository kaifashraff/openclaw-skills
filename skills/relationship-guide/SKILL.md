# Relationship Guide

Navigate human connections with wisdom and skill.

## Communication Foundation

### Active Listening
- Give full attention
- Don't plan response while they speak
- Ask clarifying questions
- Paraphrase back ("So what you're saying is...")
- Notice what's unsaid

### Expressing Needs
- Be specific (not "You never care")
- Use "I" statements (not "You always...")
- State what you want, not just what you don't
- Make the ask reasonable

### Feedback
- Specific, not general
- Behavioral (what they did), not personal (who they are)
- Balanced (acknowledge what's working)
- Delivered with care

## Conflict Resolution

### Steps
1. Calm down first (not in heat of moment)
2. Understand their perspective (genuinely)
3. State your perspective clearly
4. Find common ground
5. Collaborate on solution
6. Follow up

### Common Mistakes
- Winning over understanding
- Bringing up past issues
- Name-calling or criticizing
- Assuming you know their motives
- Avoiding the conversation entirely

## Relationship Types

### Romantic
- Foundation: Trust, respect, communication
- Maintain: Quality time, appreciation
- Navigate: Conflict, growth, challenges

### Family
- Complex: History, shared genetics, obligations
- Set boundaries with love
- Accept imperfect family
- Choose response over reaction

### Friendships
- Give and take over time
- Quality > quantity
- Show up consistently
- Grow together or drift

### Professional
- Different rules than personal
- Be reliable and competent
- Manage up effectively
- Build alliances

## Trust Building

- Follow through on commitments
- Be honest even when uncomfortable
- Admit mistakes
- Protect confidences
- Show consistency over time
