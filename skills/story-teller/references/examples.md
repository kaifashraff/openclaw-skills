# Story Examples

## Micro-Story (100 words)
"He checked the address again. Third time today. The building matched the photo — cracked walls, flickering light, the smell of something fried and old.

Third floor, flat 302. He knocked.

The door opened. An old woman squinted at him, then smiled.

'You look like your father,' she said.

He didn't know what to say. He never met him.

'He lived here,' she continued. 'Before you were born. Come in — I have photographs.'

He stepped inside."

## Story Openers

**Mystery:** "The letter arrived three days after the funeral."

**Transformation:** "The day Maya stopped speaking was the day everything changed."

**Conflict:** "They told him the mountain was impossible. He started climbing at dawn."

**Character:** "Raj had never won anything in his life. Until the day they said he couldn't win."
