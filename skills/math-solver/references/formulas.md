# Math Formulas Reference

## Algebra
- Quadratic: x = (-b ± √(b²-4ac))/2a
- Sum of n terms (AP): n/2 × (first + last)
- Sum of n terms (GP): a(r^n - 1)/(r - 1)

## Geometry
- Circle area: πr²
- Sphere volume: (4/3)πr³
- Right triangle: a² + b² = c²

## Statistics
- Mean: Σx/n
- Variance: Σ(x-x̄)²/n
- Std Dev: √variance
- Correlation: Cov(X,Y)/(σX × σY)

## Business Math
- Simple interest: P × r × t
- Compound interest: P(1 + r)^t
- Profit margin: (Revenue - Cost)/Revenue
- Break-even: Fixed Cost / (Price - Variable Cost)

## Growth
- Linear: y = mx + c
- Exponential: y = a × b^x
- Doubling time (exponential): ln(2)/ln(1+r)
