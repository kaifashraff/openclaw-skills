## Engagement Tips

- Use 3-5 relevant hashtags (mix of broad + niche)
- Include call-to-action (DM, comment, share)
- Tag location (Ahmedabad, India)
- Respond to comments within 1 hour
- Post during peak hours: 7-9 AM, 12-2 PM, 7-9 PM IST
