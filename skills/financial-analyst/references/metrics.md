# Business Metrics

## Startup Metrics
- CAC: Customer Acquisition Cost
- LTV: Lifetime Value (total revenue from customer)
- LTV:CAC ratio (target >3:1)
- MRR/ARR: Monthly/Annual Recurring Revenue

## E-commerce Metrics
- Conversion Rate: Visitors who buy
- AOV: Average Order Value
- Cart Abandonment Rate
- Repeat Purchase Rate

## Operational Metrics
- Inventory Turnover: How fast stock sells
- Debtor Days: How long to collect payment
- Creditor Days: How long to pay suppliers
- Working Capital: Current assets - current liabilities

## Valuation Multiples
- Early stage: 1-3x revenue
- Growth stage: 3-10x revenue
- Mature: 10-20x revenue
( varies by industry and growth rate )
