# Instagram Reels

Create viral-worthy Reels that showcase R Company's zari embroidery.

## Reels Content Pillars

1. **Process Reveals** (50%) — Artisan at work, embroidery close-ups, finishing touches
2. **Transformation** (25%) — Before/after, plain to styled, sketch to finished
3. **Education** (15%) — How to style, care tips, design explanations
4. **Entertainment** (10%) — Trending audio with ethnic twist, relatable moments

## Reels Ideas by Category

### Process Reveals
- "Watch 200 hours of handwork in 30 seconds" (time-lapse embroidery)
- "Our artisan's steady hands" (close-up threading)
- "From thread to masterpiece" (start-to-finish)
- "The sound of zari work" (satisfying audio)
- "Meet the hands behind your outfit"

### Transformation
- "Man in regular suit → Sherwani king" (outfit reveal)
- "Before/after: Plain fabric → Zari royalty"
- "How our bridal collection transforms brides"

### Education
- "3 ways to style one kurta"
- "What makes real zari different from machine work"
- "How to store your ethnic wear" (care tips)
- "Decoding zari: Gold vs silver vs copper"

### Trending Audio Pairings
- Trendy pop → Show craftsmanship
- Emotional → Share customer stories
- Comedy → "Things people say about our prices"
- Satisfying → Embroidery process close-ups

## Script Template

```
HOOK (0-3 sec): "You won't believe how long this takes..."
CONTEXT (3-8 sec): "This is our artisan [Name] working on..."
VALUE (8-20 sec): [Process demonstration or explanation]
CTA (20-25 sec): "Follow for more behind-the-scenes 🧵"

Total: 25-30 seconds
```

## Technical Specs

- Duration: 15-30 seconds (optimal engagement)
- Format: 9:16 (1080x1920)
- Text: Include key points (viewers watch without sound)
- Captions: Auto-generated + manual review
- Music: Use trending sounds (check weekly)
