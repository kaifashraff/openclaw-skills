## How to Find Trending Audio

1. Open Instagram Reels
2. Tap sound icon on top trending Reels
3. Check "Used by" count (higher = more trending)
4. Save to favorites for quick access
5. Refresh weekly (trends change fast!)

## Audio Sources

- Instagram trending page (refresh daily)
- Triller

- YouTube Shorts trends

## Tips

- Always test audio with draft Reels before posting
- Mix ethnic + modern for wider reach
- Use original audio when possible (higher engagement)
- Keep audio in background — shouldn't overpower narration
