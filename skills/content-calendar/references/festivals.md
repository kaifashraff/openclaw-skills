## General Festival Tips

- Post 2-3 weeks before festival
- Use festival-specific hashtags
- Include festive emojis (🪔🌙✨🎊🌸)
- Add "Order by [date] for delivery before [festival]" urgency
- Create urgency with limited stock mentions
