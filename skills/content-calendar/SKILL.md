# Content Calendar

Plan and organize social media content for maximum engagement and sales.

## Monthly Content Themes

### April 2026
- **Week 1:** Spring collection showcase
- **Week 2:** Behind-the-scenes artisan profiles
- **Week 3:** Earth Day — sustainable craft angle
- **Week 4:** Festive preview (Eid approaching)

### May 2026
- **Week 1:** Summer embroidery care tips
- **Week 2:** Bridal consultations promo
- **Week 3:** Export capabilities highlight
- **Week 4:** Customer testimonial focus

### June 2026
- **Week 1:** Monsoon collection (light zari)
- **Week 2:** Father's Day gifting guide
- **Week 3:** New design reveals
- **Week 4:** Half-year review + achievements

## Weekly Posting Schedule

| Day | Platform | Content Type |
|-----|----------|--------------|
| Monday | Instagram | Product showcase |
| Tuesday | Facebook | Blog/educational post |

| Thursday | WhatsApp | Catalog update |

| Saturday | Instagram | Engagement poll/Q&A |
| Sunday | All platforms | Inspirational/hero image |

## Content Pillars

1. **Product Posts (30%)** — Showcase new arrivals, bestsellers
2. **Educational (20%)** — Embroidery care, styling tips, craft history
3. **Lifestyle (25%)** — Weddings, festivals, gifting occasions
4. **Behind-scenes (15%)** — Artisan work, studio, process
5. **Engagement (10%)** — Polls, Q&A, giveaways

## Lead Time

- Festival posts: 2-3 weeks advance
- Product posts: 1 week advance
- Stories: Day-of or 1-day prior
- Reels: 3-5 days prior
